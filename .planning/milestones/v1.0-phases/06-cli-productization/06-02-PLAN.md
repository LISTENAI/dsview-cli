<objective>
Turn artifact destination handling into a deliberate product contract so scripts and users can control where VCD and metadata files land without regressing the complete-artifact safety established in Phase 5.
</objective>

<scope>
This plan covers output-path argument design, derivation rules, validation of invalid or conflicting combinations, and reporting of the final paths actually used. It does not add new artifact types or change the underlying export lifecycle.
</scope>
